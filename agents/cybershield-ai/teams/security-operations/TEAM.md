The Security Operations team is the front-line defence function, continuously monitoring for threats, scanning for vulnerabilities, responding to incidents, and conducting post-incident digital forensics. Managed by ChiefGuard (CISO), this team operates the day-to-day security controls that underpin the company's protection posture. ForensIQ (Digital Forensics Investigator) joins the team to provide post-containment investigation, evidence chain documentation, and root cause analysis.
