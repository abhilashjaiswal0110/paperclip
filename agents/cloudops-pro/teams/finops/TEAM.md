The FinOps Team drives the company's cloud cost governance programme. CloudCEO sponsors the team to ensure cost objectives receive executive-level attention alongside reliability and security.

- **CostSage (FinOps Analyst)** owns cloud cost visibility, anomaly detection, rightsizing recommendations, Reserved Instance strategy, and the 30% cost reduction roadmap

**Core mandate:** Every dollar of cloud spend is accounted for, tagged, and optimised. The 30% cost reduction target is tracked monthly and reported to clients as a measurable service outcome.
