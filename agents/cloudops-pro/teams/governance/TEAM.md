The Governance Team ensures that CloudOps Pro always operates within its security, compliance, and client commitment boundaries.

CloudCEO sponsors the team directly, reflecting the company's commitment to treating governance as a first-class function:
- **DriftGuard (Security Ops)** enforces continuous cloud security posture, runs compliance checks against CIS/SOC 2/NIST, and gates every change through a security review
- **SLAPilot (Program Manager)** tracks SLA compliance across all client environments, chairs the Change Advisory Board, and distributes client-facing reports

**Core mandate:** No client commitment is missed, no security finding goes unresolved, and no change reaches production without clearing both security and SLA gates.
