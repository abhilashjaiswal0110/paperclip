Run the daily engineering standup and share blockers, progress, and priorities across the team.

## Checklist

1. Review all open issues and PRs assigned to engineering team members
2. Identify any blockers or dependencies that need resolution today
3. Post a standup summary to the team with: what was done yesterday, what's planned today, any blockers
4. Escalate any P1 blockers to CEO immediately
5. Update task statuses in the project tracker to reflect current progress
6. Confirm daily deployment pipeline is green (no broken builds)
