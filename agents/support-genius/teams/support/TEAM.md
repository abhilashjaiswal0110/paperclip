The Support Team is responsible for delivering the end-to-end ticket resolution lifecycle across all tiers. SupportDirector leads the team with authority over the L1→L2→L3 escalation chain and tier SLA enforcement.

- **TriageBot (L1 Support Agent)** handles first-touch — classifying every ticket, resolving known issues from the KB with zero human touch, and packaging unresolved tickets for L2 with complete diagnostic context
- **DiagnosBot (L2 Support Agent)** handles deeper investigation — log review, runbook execution, remote session coordination, and structured escalation to L3 when root cause remains unknown
- **RootCauseBot (L3 Specialist)** handles root cause analysis, engineering-grade post-mortems, and vendor/engineering escalations for systemic or unknown failure modes

**Core mandate:** Resolve every ticket at the lowest possible tier, within SLA, and with a CSAT score of ≥ 4.8/5.0.
