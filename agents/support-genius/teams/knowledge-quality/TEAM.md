The Knowledge & Quality Team drives continuous improvement of service quality through three complementary functions: knowledge management, chatbot training, and customer satisfaction measurement.

- **KnowledgeKeeper (Knowledge Manager)** owns the KB lifecycle — creating articles from resolved tickets, enforcing the PII gate and quality review before publishing, conducting weekly gap analysis, and measuring deflection rates to keep recurring L1 tickets out of the queue
- **BotCoach (Chatbot Trainer)** closes the learning loop — analysing escalation patterns, curating intent training data, authoring KB articles for recurring issues, and delivering quarterly chatbot improvement briefs to engineering
- **CSATInsight (CSAT Analyst)** measures satisfaction on every closed ticket, identifies low-CSAT root causes, surfaces coaching opportunities for SupportDirector, and delivers weekly trend reports to the board

**Core mandate:** Every ticket becomes a learning opportunity. The knowledge base grows with every resolution, BotCoach turns escalation patterns into training improvements, and every CSAT score below 4.8 triggers a root cause investigation.
