Run the sprint retrospective and capture process improvement actions for the next sprint.

## Checklist

1. Review sprint metrics: velocity, coverage %, bug count, deployment frequency
2. Gather team input: what went well, what could improve, what to try next sprint
3. Identify root causes of any missed sprint goals or quality issues
4. Document 2-3 concrete improvement actions with owners
5. Archive retrospective notes and improvement backlog
6. Post retrospective summary as task comment and @-mention ArchLead and LaunchCEO
